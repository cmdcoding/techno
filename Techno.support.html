Laggy/Glitchy/Distorted Audio

Sometimes, Techno's audio may sound laggy,glitchy and distorted. The vast majority of the time, this is caused by Discord's voice servers.

The easiest way to fix it is to switch your voice server region to a different one and then to switch it back.

Trouble shooting

Techno not responding?

Is Discord having issues?
Check Discord's status page. If there is an ongoing incident, it is likely that the issue is caused by the outage.

Is Techno online?
Check the sidebar to see if Techno is online. If techno appears as offline, then something has probably gone wrong. Please immediately contact a support member in the Support  section of are disocrd.
